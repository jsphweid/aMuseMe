aMuseMe

The whole point of this is to stimulate creativity. Just go. Don't think. Use your intuition. Who cares what you write? You can always change it later. Just write something!

Get your ideas down quickly. This is not an editor. This is designed solely to get stuff of paper quickly (metaphorically speaking). Once you get your ideas down, you can easily copy/paste your work away.

If you are logged in as a Google user, all of your sessions are saved. You can delete a session at any time during the session or later.

This project is hosted on GitHub.
