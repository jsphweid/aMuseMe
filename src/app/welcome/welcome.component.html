aMuseMe

The whole point of this is to stimulate creativity. Just go. Don't think. Use your intuition. Who cares what you write? You can always change it later. Just write something!

Get your ideas down quickly. This is not an editor. This is designed solely to get stuff of paper quickly (metaphorically speaking). Once you get your ideas down, you can easily copy/paste your work away or use export options.

By default, everything you type is saved, even when you exit. You can delete a session at any time during the session or later if you are logged in or have your key.

HAVING SAID ALL OF THAT, this app is experimental and your data could be lost if I decide to make drastic changes to the structure. You've been warned.
